Privacy Policy – Vizit

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Vizit ("we", "our", or "us") is a contact scanning app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Images: Photos you take or select are sent to our secure proxy server to extract contact information using AI. Images are not stored on our servers.
  • Contact data: Extracted contact information is stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase data: Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment information.

2. Permissions Used

  • Camera: To photograph business cards and documents.
  • Photo Library: To import images from your gallery.
  • Contacts: To save extracted contact information to your address book (only when you explicitly choose to save).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Images are processed via a Cloudflare Worker proxy and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API solely to extract contact information. No data is retained after processing.

4. Data Retention

All contact history is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time from the app settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Vizit is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
